Design Of Experiments#

This tutorial shows how you can use PyAdditive to run a design of experiments (DOE). For this DOE we will vary laser power and scan speed over multiple single bead simulations and plot the results.

Units are SI (m, kg, s, K) unless otherwise noted.

First, connect to the Additive service.
