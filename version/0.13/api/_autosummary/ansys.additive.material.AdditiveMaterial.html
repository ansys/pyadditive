AdditiveMaterial#

class ansys.additive.material.AdditiveMaterial(*, absorptivity_maximum=0, absorptivity_minimum=0, absorptivity_powder_coefficient_a=0, absorptivity_powder_coefficient_b=0, absorptivity_solid_coefficient_a=0, absorptivity_solid_coefficient_b=0, anisotropic_strain_coefficient_parallel=0, anisotropic_strain_coefficient_perpendicular=0, anisotropic_strain_coefficient_z=0, elastic_modulus=0, hardening_factor=0, liquidus_temperature=0, material_yield_strength=0, name='', nucleation_constant_bulk=0, nucleation_constant_interface=0, penetration_depth_maximum=0, penetration_depth_minimum=0, penetration_depth_powder_coefficient_a=0, penetration_depth_powder_coefficient_b=0, penetration_depth_solid_coefficient_a=0, penetration_depth_solid_coefficient_b=0, poisson_ratio=0, powder_packing_density=0, purging_gas_convection_coefficient=0, solid_density_at_room_temperature=0, solid_specific_heat_at_room_temperature=0, solid_thermal_conductivity_at_room_temperature=0, solidus_temperature=0, strain_scaling_factor=0, support_yield_strength_ratio=0, thermal_expansion_coefficient=0, vaporization_temperature=0, characteristic_width_data=None, thermal_properties_data=None)#

Container for material properties used during additive manufacturing simulation.

Methods

Attributes

AdditiveMaterial.absorptivity_maximum

Absorptivity maximum.

AdditiveMaterial.absorptivity_minimum

Absorptivity minimum.

AdditiveMaterial.absorptivity_powder_coefficient_a

Absorptivity powder coefficient a.

AdditiveMaterial.absorptivity_powder_coefficient_b

Absorptivity powder coefficient b.

AdditiveMaterial.absorptivity_solid_coefficient_a

Absorptivity solid coefficient a.

AdditiveMaterial.absorptivity_solid_coefficient_b

Absorptivity solid coefficient b.

AdditiveMaterial.anisotropic_strain_coefficient_parallel

Multiplier on the predicted strain in the direction that the laser is scanning for the major fill rasters.

AdditiveMaterial.anisotropic_strain_coefficient_perpendicular

Multiplier on the predicted strain orthogonal to the direction that the laser is scanning for the major fill rasters and in the plane of the surface of the build plate.

AdditiveMaterial.anisotropic_strain_coefficient_z

Multiplier on the predicted strain in the Z direction.

AdditiveMaterial.characteristic_width_data

List of CharacteristicWidthDataPoint.

AdditiveMaterial.elastic_modulus

Elastic modulus (Pa).

AdditiveMaterial.hardening_factor

Factor relating the elastic modulus to the tangent modulus for plasticity simulations (tangent modulus = elastic modulus * hardening factor ).

AdditiveMaterial.liquidus_temperature

Minimum temperature at which the material is completely liquid (K).

AdditiveMaterial.material_yield_strength

Material yield strength (Pa).

AdditiveMaterial.name

Name of material.

AdditiveMaterial.nucleation_constant_bulk

Controls the homogeneous nucleation rate (in bulk of the microstructure simulation domain) during solidification (1/m^2/K^2).

AdditiveMaterial.nucleation_constant_interface

Controls the heterogeneous nucleation rate (on existing solid interfaces) during solidification (1/m/K^2).

AdditiveMaterial.penetration_depth_maximum

Penetration depth maximum.

AdditiveMaterial.penetration_depth_minimum

Penetration depth minimum.

AdditiveMaterial.penetration_depth_powder_coefficient_a

Penetration depth powder coefficient a.

AdditiveMaterial.penetration_depth_powder_coefficient_b

Penetration depth powder coefficient b.

AdditiveMaterial.penetration_depth_solid_coefficient_a

Penetration depth solid coefficient a.

AdditiveMaterial.penetration_depth_solid_coefficient_b

Penetration depth solid coefficient b.

AdditiveMaterial.poisson_ratio

Poisson ratio.

AdditiveMaterial.powder_packing_density

Density of powder material relative to the solid.

AdditiveMaterial.purging_gas_convection_coefficient

Convection coefficient between the solid and gas during processing.

AdditiveMaterial.solid_density_at_room_temperature

Density of bulk material at room temperature, 298 K (kg/m^3).

AdditiveMaterial.solid_specific_heat_at_room_temperature

Specific heat of bulk material at room temperature, 298 K (J/kg/K).

AdditiveMaterial.solid_thermal_conductivity_at_room_temperature

Thermal conductivity of bulk material at room temperature, 298 K (W/m/K).

AdditiveMaterial.solidus_temperature

Maximum temperature at which the material is completely solid (K).

AdditiveMaterial.strain_scaling_factor

Strain scaling factor.

AdditiveMaterial.support_yield_strength_ratio

Factor to reduce the yield strength and elastic modulus of support material.

AdditiveMaterial.thermal_expansion_coefficient

Coefficient of thermal expansion (1/K).

AdditiveMaterial.thermal_properties_data

List of ThermalPropertiesDataPoint.

AdditiveMaterial.vaporization_temperature

Temperature at which material has completely changed from liquid to vapor (K).
